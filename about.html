Hi, I'm Glynnis.

I’m a multidisciplinary freelance designer with 10+ years of experience in everything from traditional graphic design, UI/UX design, and design for web and mobile apps.

I love digging deep to understand complex problems and user behavior. I focus on creating modern, user-centered designs that are accessible, inclusive, and distinctive.

My career in tech began with software development, and I feel at home working with engineers to understand technical limitations and find solutions that can be implemented iteratively without compromising creative vision or user experience.

I've managed design teams at small companies, worked with start-ups and non-profits, and led work on design systems at a large enterprise company.

When I’m not designing, you can find me at the clay studio throwing or handbuilding ceramics inspired by the colors and tropical flora of New Orleans.

What I do

  • Design ranging from print to UX research to visual design
  • Integrate product managers, engineers, and stakeholders into the design process
  • Explain the “why” of design decisions

My Process

  • Observe and articulate complex problems
  • Sketch and distill concepts into solid solutions
  • Vet technical feasibility with engineering to define iterative steps and releases
  • Test and solicit feedback with users
