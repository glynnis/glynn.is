Hi, I'm Glynnis.

I’m a UX Designer at Boundless, an open source platform for location-based data. Previously, I led design on the EncoreUI Framework, a design system and component library for internal tools at Rackspace.

I’m passionate about creative collaboration, interdisciplinary teams, and the spaces where elegant code and delightful experiences overlap.

When I’m not designing for the web, I design books and print collateral for Big Class, a New Orleans nonprofit that cultivates and supports voices of writers aged 6 to 18. People also know me know me as a calligrapher, tea connoisseur, and tabletop gaming nerd.

What I do

  • Full stack design ranging from UX research to  visual design
  • Integrate product managers, engineers, and stakeholders into the design process
  • Explain the “why” of design decisions

My Process

  • Observe and articulate complex problems
  • Sketch and distill concepts into solid solutions
  • Vet technical feasibility with engineering to define iterative steps and releases
  • Write CSS and pair with engineers to bridge divide between design and engineering skillsets
  • Test and solicit feedback with users

Related Creative Pursuits

  • Lived in Paris and studied French at the Sorbonne
  • Sorted movable type and cleaned letterpresses at the Center for Book Arts in New York City
  • Photographed film industry press conferences and panels for the American Pavilian at the Cannes Film Festival
  • Co-founded a successful video production business
